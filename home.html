About Us

Welcome to Getaways tours!

Getaways tours is established to provide you a different aspect of sightseeing , hiking tours and a variety of activities based on small groups of maximum 11 participants. Our mission is to provide guests with high quality mainstream as well as alternative tours that showcase the beauty and heritage of Athens and Greece . Our carefully selected tours performed exclusively by minivan of no more than 11 passengers are designed to offer you privacy and personal customer care. Moreover all tours are escorted by professional guides to ensure your day out is one to remember. Take your friend and/or your family and get ready to meet different people on board and get ready to explore this amazing city and surroundings.

Just Relax and enjoy your stay in our beautiful country!

One Day Tour To Delphi And Arachova (9 Hours) Max 11 Persons

Price: 85.00

One Day Tour To Delphi And Arachova (9 Hours) Max 11 Persons

Depart from your hotel early in the morning.Drive through the fertile plain of Beotia, crossing the towns of Thebes - connected with the tragedy of King Oedipus and the city of Levadia in which we will have a rest stop. Arrive at Delphi known in ancient times as the navel of the world, the "Omphalos," , whose prestige extended far beyond the boundaries of the Hellenic World. Pass by Castalia Spring, visit at the archaeological site and view the monument of the Argive Kings, the treasury of the Athenians, the Athenian Stoa, the Polygonal Wall, the monument of Platea and the Temple of Apollo famous for its oracle. Entrance to Delphi museum, with spectacular exhibits including the frieze of the Treasury of the Sifnians, the Naxian Sphinx, the Statue of Antinoos , the famous bronze Charioteer, and many other artifacts. Optional lunch at the modern village of Delphi and return to Athens via the picturesque mountain village of Arachova ( short stop ), built on the south slopes of Parnassus.

This tour returns to Athens in the late afternoon dropping you off at your hotels.

Included

Transport by air-conditioned mini van Pick up / drop off from Athens hotels English speaking guide departing from Athens Guided tour in Delphi and the museum All taxes, fees and handling charges
